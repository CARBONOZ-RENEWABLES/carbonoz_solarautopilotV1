About Enhanced Dynamic Pricing

                    

                    

                        

                            

                                
 Smart Price Thresholds

                                
Set your maximum acceptable price per kWh. The system only charges when electricity prices fall below your threshold, ensuring cost-effective battery charging.

                            

                            

                                
 Weather Intelligence

                                
Integrates weather forecasts to charge before storms or cloudy periods. Uses OpenWeatherMap API to predict solar generation and optimize charging timing.

                            

                            

                                
 Power-Based Conditions

                                
Optional conditions based on current load, PV generation, and battery power. Fine-tune when charging should occur based on your energy usage patterns.

                            

                            

                                
 Cooldown Management

                                
Prevents excessive charging cycles with configurable cooldown periods and daily limits. Protects your battery while maintaining intelligent charging behavior.

                            

                            

                                
 Intelligent Decision Making

                                
Combines all conditions intelligently - price thresholds, weather forecasts, power states, and time preferences to make optimal charging decisions.

                            

                            

                                
 Safety & Control

                                
Multiple safety layers including emergency charging at low SoC, maximum SoC limits, and manual override options. You maintain full control at all times.
